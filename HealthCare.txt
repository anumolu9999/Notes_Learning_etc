RxBIN/RxPCN/RxGrp - 
Sometimes the medical insurance company does not cover prescriptions but prints the information of the prescription insurance company that does cover it on the same card.

Regardless of the insurance company, the pharmacy is looking for four pieces of necessary information.
RxBin - the Bank Identification Number routes a claim towards the right insurance
RxPCN - the Processor Control Number further narrows the claim to the specific insurance
ID - the member's designated identification number
RxGrp - the prescription group number denotes which group plan the member ID falls under

--------------------------------------------- 4
Claim Lifecycle - End to End Claim Flow

Patien Registration 
        - Register for service/Book appointment for visit or treatment
Eligibility Verification 
        - member verfied through EDI 270 request by validating EDI 271 response
Service Provision 
        - Services will be rendered for patient
Charge Capture 
        - capture quantity and charge for the diagnosis(tests) and treatment(surgery/care)
Coding
        -       For diagnosis like related tests (ICD10)
                for treatment like sugeries (CPT/HCPCS)
        Provider side
                HL7.V2 - Lab ordering made by provider is done by HL7.V2
                Snowmed
                Loinc
                Dicom
        But Payer require only ICD10/CPT-HCPCS codes

Claim Creation: 
        Patient Info| 
                if dependent then Subscriber Info also)
        Provider Info |
                (Individual FN,LN,NPI1,Taxonomy/Entity FN,NPI2,)
                Provider can have multiple taxonomy - They can submit primary taxonomy
                if cardiac surgery is done they have to submit the respective taxonomy
        Clinical Info|
                ICD10
                CPT/HCPCS
        Financial Info| 
                - Qty Charge

Claim Submission:
        CMS1500 -HCFA(paperclaims) - EDI 837P  for professional claim -Clinical (ICD10+CPT/HCPCS)
                                        837P asks for Billing provider/Rendering provider/attending provider
        UB04 -CMS1450(paperclaims) - EDI 837I for Institutional/facility claim - Institutional (ICD10+Revendue Codes)
        ADA claim form             - EDI 837D for Dental claim

Clearing House Processing: Any conversions(paper to EDI), errors, corrections will be reviewed  
        JMS (Just Make sense -one of the clearing house to convert paper to EDI)
        Availity, Smart Data Solutions, DentalXChange, FinThrive, PNT Data, HFMI

Payer System: Receive Claim in Administrative System(Facets/QNXT/HealthRule Payer)
                /Verify Eligibility and benefit level
                /Apply Pricing Claim Edits
                        Basic Edits and Detailed Edits  based on
                                - contracted max allowed
                                - propretary claim edits (insurer specific/repricing/codes claims are eligible for payment or not)

Adjudication
        Adjust the claims based on the edits and eligibility

Determination
        Determines final payment for the claim after edits. Added to 'To be paid' and to payment system

Payment/Denial
        claim deneied for in-eligible codes
        payment will be processed for elibiles

Provider Posting
        Payer sends the EOB/RA to provider and patient. 
                Details about the amount paid for the eligible services
                in-eligible list which got rejected
Patient Billing
        if any amount not paid by payer for in-eligible codes, will be billed to patient

Appeals (if Required)
        if any eligibles are rejected, can be appealed

Resolution
        if any appeals - Valid appeals > Processed and Paid, Invalid>rejected
