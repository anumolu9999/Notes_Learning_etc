RxBin - the Bank Identification Number routes a claim towards the right insurance
RxPCN - the Processor Control Number further narrows the claim to the specific insurance

RxGrp - the prescription group number denotes which group plan the member ID falls under

RVU's
In healthcare, Relative Value Units (RVUs) are not a single formula but a scaled value assigned to each medical service, calculated as the 
sum of three components: 
    Work RVUs, 
    Practice Expense (PE) RVUs, and 
    Malpractice (MP) RVUs. 
The total RVU for a service is then multiplied by a Conversion Factor to determine the payment for that service.

ICD-10 created by CMS and NCHS ((National Center for Health Statistics)
ICD-10 divided/consist of two medical code sets
1. Clinical Modification (ICD-10-CM) - Across all claims (Prof/inst IP/OP)
          Standardised classification system of diagnosis codes that represent conditions and           disease, related health problems, abnoramal findings, signs and symptoms, injuries,            external causes of injuries and diseases, and social circumstances
2. Procedure Coding System (ICD-10-PCS)
          Classification system of medical codes used in hospital settings to report          inpatient procedures
